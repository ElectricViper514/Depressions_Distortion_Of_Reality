My Journey

Hello, I'm a 49-year-old computer science student, aspiring developer, and mental health advocate. After decades of struggling with depression, I discovered that coding, gardening, and continuous learning became powerful tools in my healing process.

This blog documents my journey of overcoming depression's distorted reality through faith, creative expression, nature, and technology. I share my experiences learning to walk in faith, cultivating my garden, and returning to school to pursue my passion for computer science.

My goal is to inspire others who may be facing similar challenges and to demonstrate that it's never too late to pursue your dreams and rewrite your story.
